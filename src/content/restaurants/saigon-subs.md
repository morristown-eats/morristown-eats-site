Banh mi on Elm Street. No-frills, counter-service, and the Vietnamese iced coffee is the real reason to come — condensed milk, strong as hell, poured over ice. The kind of coffee that actually wakes you up.

The Saigon Special is the move. Freshly toasted roll, pâté, mayo, pickled carrots and daikon, cilantro, jalapeños. Summer rolls, entrées, bubble tea if you want something different.

Closed Sundays. Get there before 7.
