The Address is the newest of the South Street BYOB restaurants, having opened in 2024, and it fills a specific gap: the date-night table on a block where the options have historically skewed either casual or inconsistent.

Greek-Mediterranean menu, a full bar alongside the BYOB option, and a heated patio that extends the season. The combination makes it the right answer for a particular kind of Friday night — something with a reservation, a bottle from home, and a reason to linger.

Adjacent to Coniglio's and Dän Bakery, making the South Street triple-play a realistic afternoon: coffee and a pastry at Dän, pizza at Coniglio's for lunch, dinner at The Address with a bottle.
