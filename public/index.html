About

What this is

A local guide built on actual visits. No sponsored listings, no pay-to-play, no comment sections. Just the restaurants worth knowing about, written by someone who eats here and pays for his own food.

What it isn't

It's not Yelp. It's not a complaint platform. It's not chain-first or advertiser-first. Silence is the negative review. If a place isn't listed, it either hasn't been visited yet or isn't worth your time.
